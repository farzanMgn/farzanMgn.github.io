# Farzaneh Moghani  

I am a **computer engineer** with a strong foundation in **data science** and **product development**.  

My journey began in Iran, where I was admitted to the country's elite high school for exceptional talents. Later, I earned my **Bachelor’s degree in Computer Engineering** from **Ferdowsi University of Mashhad**, one of the top institutions in eastern Iran. During my time there, I served as the **Head of the Computer Association**, gaining hands-on experience in **leadership, event management, and cross-functional collaboration**.  

### Research & Professional Experience  

For my undergraduate thesis, I worked on a **robotic-assisted physiotherapy system** in collaboration with **Mashhad University of Medical Sciences**, developing an **Android application** to interact with the robotic system. This interdisciplinary project introduced me to the **intersection of technology and healthcare**, sparking my interest in real-world problem-solving.  

After graduation, I joined **iTele**, a startup where I gained experience in **UX research**. Seeking to expand my knowledge, I moved to Italy for my **Master’s in Computer Engineering at the University of Pisa**, specializing in **Cyber-Physical Systems**. During my studies, I was honored to receive the **Best Merit Award for Foreign Students**, recognizing my dedication and academic excellence.  

Alongside my studies, I worked as a **Data Scientist at Zerynth**, an **IoT company**, where I analyzed **time-series power consumption data** to optimize **industrial energy usage**. My work involved:  
- **Machine learning**  
- **Anomaly detection**  
- **Data visualization**  

These efforts contributed to helping businesses make **data-driven decisions**.
